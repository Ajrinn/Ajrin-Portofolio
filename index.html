About

The person behind the pixels

Since 2018

Say hi. I'm Ajrin, a Product Designer based in Jakarta who turns messy ideas into clean, usable products.

Curious soul, powered by coffee. Over the past 6+ years I've helped startups and companies turn complex problems into simple, usable, and delightful products across web and mobile - caring about the details without losing sight of the people using them.

The Journey

From Informatics Management at Politeknik Negeri Sriwijaya, to Graphic Designer, into a deliberate transition to UI/UX - and now leading design as a UI/UX Lead. A path from making things look good to making things work well.
